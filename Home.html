About Our Panchayat

Gambhira Gram Panchayat is a Rural Local Body in Nainwa Panchayat Samiti part of Bundi Zila Parishad. There are total 8 Villages under Gambhira Gram Panchayat jurisdiction. Gram Panchayat Nainwa is further divided into 11 Wards. Gram Panchayat Nainwa has total 12 elected members by people. Nainwa Gram Panchayat has total 6 full time government employees.
